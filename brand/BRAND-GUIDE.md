# The Super 7 — Brand Guide

**Tagline:** *The Home of the Super 7*

A football media/content brand centered on England's "big seven" Premier League
clubs: Arsenal, Chelsea, Liverpool, Manchester City, Manchester United,
Newcastle United, and Tottenham Hotspur.

**Personality:** Bold · Premium · Broadcast-grade · Sporty · Modern

## Usage Rules

- **Yellow is the hero.** Use it for the logo, headlines, key stats, and primary
  CTAs — not for large background fills (it's high-energy, use it sparingly).
- **Charcoal & Ink** carry most surfaces. The brand lives in dark mode by default.
- **Chrome Silver** is the "metallic" voice — dividers, secondary labels, the
  "THE SUPER" wordmark style, subtle borders.
- **Contrast:** Yellow on Charcoal/Ink passes for large/bold text. For body copy,
  use Off-White on dark, or Ink on light.

---

## Typography (recommended)

- **Display / Headlines:** A bold condensed sport face — e.g. *Anton*,
  *Archivo Expanded*, or *Teko*. All-caps, tight tracking (matches "THE HOME OF
  THE SUPER 7").
- **Body / UI:** A clean grotesque — e.g. *Inter*, *Roboto*, or *Manrope*.
- **Accent / "THE SUPER" style:** italic, condensed, uppercase, letter-spaced.

---

## Logo

- 3D angular interlocking monogram (TS / dual-7) in glossy Super Yellow with
  Chrome Silver beveled edges.
- Clear space: keep at least the height of the "7" around all sides.
- Backgrounds: place on Charcoal or Ink. Avoid busy or light backgrounds unless
  using a flat/mono version.
- Minimum size: keep the "7" legible — don't render the full mark below ~40px tall.
